## How It Works

1. **Initialization**: Center tile starts as vacuum (black), surrounding tiles have normal air (green)
2. **Gas Flow**: Each frame, the system calculates pressure differentials
3. **Equalization**: Gas flows from high pressure to low pressure tiles
4. **Visual Update**: Tile colors update in real-time to reflect pressure changes
5. **Convergence**: Eventually all tiles reach equilibrium (all green)

## Interactive Features

- Press **SPACE** to print detailed atmospheric data to console
- Gas sharing happens automatically every frame
- Heat transfer also occurs (not visible in this demo)
